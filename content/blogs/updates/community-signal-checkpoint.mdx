# Community Signal Checkpoint

Community signal matters most when it is specific enough to influence the next product decision.

## What we are hearing

Early adopters want clearer launch expectations, more examples of supported flows, and fewer ambiguous beta-only promises.

## How it changes the roadmap

That feedback pushes us toward shorter public notes, stronger release framing, and more visible examples of real integrations.

## What to expect

Expect future update posts to tie feedback themes directly to changes in docs, product copy, and launch sequencing.
