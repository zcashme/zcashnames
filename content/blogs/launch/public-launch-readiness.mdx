# Public Launch Readiness

Readiness is not just about shipping the feature set. It is about whether the surrounding messaging makes the launch legible.

## Product readiness

The registry flow, pricing explanation, and user support path all need to feel coherent at the same time.

## Market readiness

Launch notes should explain who the first users are, why the initial price exists, and what changes after beta assumptions end.

## Communication readiness

The team should be able to point to one concise source for rollout status, launch caveats, and expected next milestones.
