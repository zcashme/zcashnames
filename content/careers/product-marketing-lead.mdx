# Fractional Product Marketing Lead

## Purpose

Turn production launch, integrations, and releases into clear market communication. Explain ZcashNames to users, developers, wallets, and ecosystem partners without pulling founders into a full-time marketing operation.

## Responsibilities

- Define positioning, messaging, and audience segments.
- Create production launch materials and integration announcements.
- Turn docs, demos, and releases into developer-facing content.
- Improve website copy, use-case pages, and partner-facing materials.
- Package integrations into case studies.
- Track which messages drive interest, integrations, and usage.

## Required

- Product marketing experience for developer tools, infrastructure, wallets, privacy, crypto, or open-source products.
- Clear writing.
- Ability to understand technical products without flattening the details.
- Ability to work asynchronously with minimal process.

## Strong Plus

- Zcash or privacy ecosystem context.
- Developer marketing or DevRel content background.
- Experience launching integrations and coordinating with partners.

## Success

- Production launch story is clear.
- Website explains the product and integration path.
- Releases are announced consistently and accurately.
- Founders spend less time writing launch materials.
